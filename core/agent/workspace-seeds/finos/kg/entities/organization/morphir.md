A universal language/IR for business and technology logic. 200 stars, core in Rust.
Many language-binding repos: morphir-elm, morphir-jvm, morphir-scala, morphir-dotnet,
morphir-rust, morphir-go, morphir-python, morphir-gleam, morphir-zig, morphir-moonbit,
plus morphir-examples and legend-integration-morphir.

Source: https://github.com/finos/morphir
