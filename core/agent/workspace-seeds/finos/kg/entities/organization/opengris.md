title: OpenGRIS
---

Standard for allowing different hardware/compute pools to be efficiently shared
across HPC workloads. Related repos: htc-grid (66 stars), opengris-scaler
(distributed computation engine, 37 stars), opengris-pargraph, opengris-parfun,
open-resource-broker, 5-spot (Kubernetes spot-scheduling controller, donated to
FINOS by [[Erick Bourgeois]]). [[Boris Litvin]] (TOC candidate, [[AWS]]) is an
active contributor to opengris-scaler.

Source: https://github.com/finos/opengris
