Chair of the [[Technical Oversight Committee]] at [[FINOS]] (term until Feb 25, 2027).
Works at [[Morgan Stanley]]. GitHub: @psmulovics

## Role

Executive Director at [[Morgan Stanley]], leading Windows and .NET development
practices and spatial computing / metaverse development practices. Joined Morgan
Stanley after a decade-plus at Microsoft, where he worked on .NET, C#, Azure, Visual
Studio, SQL Server, Dynamics, and HoloLens.

## FINOS involvement

- Chair, [[Technical Oversight Committee]] (term until Feb 25, 2027).
- Co-chair of two FINOS SIGs: [[Open Source Readiness Initiative]] and the
  [[Emerging Technologies SIG]] (covers Quantum Technology, Spatial Computing,
  Space Tech, 4D printing, and related areas).
- Named a FINOS Ambassador (Linux Foundation) and a Microsoft Most Valuable
  Professional (MVP).
- Won the 2023 FINOS Community Award for "Spread The Word," recognizing
  contributions showing open source extends beyond code.
- Main organizer of the FSI Autism Hackathon.
