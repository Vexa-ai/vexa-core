title: Common Domain Model (CDM)
---

A standardized, machine-readable and machine-executable model representing
financial products, trades, and their lifecycle events (261 stars). Built on the
Rune DSL (rune-dsl, rune-common, rune-testing, rune-python-generator,
rune-python-runtime) and cdm-object-builder. [[Jon Freedman]] cited buy-side
experience with CDM (and FDC3) as part of his TOC candidacy.

Source: https://github.com/finos/common-domain-model
