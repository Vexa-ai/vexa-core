FINOS's largest open-source project (1,515 stars on the flagship `legend` repo) — a
data management and modeling platform. Component repos: legend-engine, legend-studio,
legend-pure, legend-sdlc, legend-depot, legend-shared, legend-community-delta, plus
several Juju/charm operators for K8s deployment.

Top legend-engine contributor: [[Rafael Bey-Hernandez]] ([[Goldman Sachs]]). Many
contributor handles carry a `-gs` suffix, reflecting Legend's origin at Goldman Sachs
before its donation to FINOS.
